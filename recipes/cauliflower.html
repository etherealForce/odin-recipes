Garlic Mashed Cauliflower

Description

The author said this recipe is a perfect low-card side dish. Plus he also compared it to mashed potatoes. The author also mentioned that he created it after he couldn't have mashed potatoes.The author recommends a large food processor for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, smashed
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-fat cream cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
